What is Ancient and Modern Warfare Facts

A page created to bring random facts, strategies reviews, breaking news in the military world and about any information about ancient and modern warfare.

Our mission is To educate people about warfare in general, from facts to myths, we share about anything controversial or not.Whenever something new happens we try to post it as soon as possible without spamming people too much, so we stick to 2 - 4 posts a day.When nothing happens we post some history related war posts

We do not take sides,just sharing history.
