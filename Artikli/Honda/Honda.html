Honda CRX Si 1984 commerical.

A historically unique Honda ad for the 1989 Honda CRX Si, which first ran in October 1988.

The ad video and poster showed a surprisingly accurate mock-up of the Northrop Grumman B-2 Spirit stealth bomberâ€¦ which was actually unveiled to the public several weeks AFTER the Honda ad had been first shown - in late November 1988.

It is not fully certain where Honda got information on the overall look of the top-secret military development, some sources stated it came from recruitment ads, some sources stated it came from concept sketches that Northrop Grumman made available

Whatever the source may have been, Honda took its ad seriously - what you see is actually a 3/5 scale, 9-ton, 30-meter-wingspan sheet-metal-and-steel-frame mock-up of the stealth bomber, built for around $200 000 in California, then shipped in sections to Florida to be filmed for the ad on an Everglades runway designed initially for the supersonic Concorde airliner.

The model was made so well, that it was even featured as a piece at the Strategic Air Command Museum, by Ellsworth Air Force Base, South Dakota, between 1989 and 2004.

Unfortunately, in 2004 the ad mock-up was scrapped, to make space for a (real) B-1B Lancer, as per the original agreement with Honda.
